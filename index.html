About Us

Sri Subramanya Educational Institutions is a trusted name in student coaching and development, located in Vinjamur, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh. We are dedicated to providing a high-quality learning environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. Our experienced faculty and well-structured curriculum are designed to help students achieve their full potential and secure top ranks in competitive exams. We believe in a holistic approach to education, preparing our students not just for exams, but for a brighter future.

Yoga:YOGA

At Sri Subramanya Educational Institutions, we believe that true success comes from a balanced mind and body. That's why we integrate the practice of **yoga and meditation** into our curriculum.

We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where our students can thrive, and our yoga program is a testament to this holistic approach to education.

Our Daily Activities

A Typical Day at Sri Subramanya

Our structured daily schedule is designed to maximize student focus and learning, ensuring a balanced approach to studies, rest, and personal growth.

  • **Morning Session (5:00 AM - 8:30 AM):** Intensive morning study and revision sessions.
  • **Morning Classes (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM):** Core subject coaching and concept building.
  • **Afternoon Session (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM):** Doubt clearing, practice tests, and one-on-one student-teacher interaction.
  • **Evening Session (6:00 PM - 9:00 PM):** Guided self-study and comprehensive review of the day's topics.

EAMCET Short-Term Coaching

Our intensive EAMCET coaching program is designed for aspiring engineers and medical professionals. We cover all essential subjects, including **Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology**, with a focus on problem-solving techniques and time management. Our experienced faculty provide personalized guidance and conduct regular mock tests to ensure students are fully prepared for the competitive exam environment.

Navodaya Coaching (4th & 5th Class)

This foundation course is meticulously crafted to help students crack the JNVST entrance exam. We focus on building a strong base in **Mental Ability, Arithmetic, and Language**. Our teaching methodology is designed to instill a solid understanding of core concepts, ensuring that students from both rural and urban areas are well-prepared for the exam. We prepare students from **4th class** for early advantage and **5th class** for focused exam preparation.

Sainik School Coaching (8th Class)

Our specialized program for Sainik School entrance exams covers all aspects of the test, including the written exam, physical training, and interview preparation. We focus on subjects like **Mathematics, General Knowledge, English, and Intelligence**. Our goal is to prepare students to not only pass the exam but also excel in the disciplined and challenging environment of a Sainik school.
